TLScontact & Capago: Language Requirements for UK and France Student Visas

Applying for a student visa in the UK or France involves strict bureaucratic processes managed by external agencies like TLScontact and Capago in Algeria. One of the most common reasons for dossier rejection is a misunderstood language proficiency requirement. our premier IELTS preparation in Oran facility provides the tactical blueprint to secure your target band.

Critical Warning:

If you are applying for a UK Tier 4 Visa, the standard Academic IELTS is not enough. You must explicitly book the IELTS for UKVI (Academic). Submitting a standard IELTS certificate at TLScontact will result in an automatic visa refusal and loss of your application fee.

The UK Student Visa (Tier 4) via TLScontact

To study at a degree level or above in the UK, your university must issue you a CAS (Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies). To get an unconditional CAS, you need to prove your English proficiency.

1

Book UKVI IELTS

The UKVI IELTS is recorded on video and has stricter security protocols. The format is identical to the standard IELTS, but it generates a unique UKVI number on your Test Report Form (TRF).

2

Upload to TLScontact Portal

Scan your UKVI IELTS certificate in high resolution. When you book your biometric appointment, upload this scan in the "Educational Documents" category.

France Campus France & Capago

While France is obviously a Francophone destination requiring TCF or DELF/DALF for most programs, the landscape is changing. Many prestigious Grandes Écoles and public universities now offer 100% English-taught Master's degrees.

  • You are generally exempt from the TCF DAP or TCF TP.
  • You must provide an Academic IELTS score (usually 6.5 minimum).
  • Unlike the UK, France accepts the standard Academic IELTS. You do not need a UKVI version.
