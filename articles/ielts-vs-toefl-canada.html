IELTS vs. TEF/TCF: Maximizing Your Canada Express Entry Points

For Algerian professionals seeking immigration to Canada via Express Entry or provincial nominee programs, maximizing your CRS (Comprehensive Ranking System) score is vital. The language test is the single fastest way to boost your profile. our premier IELTS preparation in Oran facility provides the tactical blueprint to secure your target band.

The Bilingual Bonus Strategy

Since most Algerians have a strong background in French, many assume the TEF or TCF Canada is enough. However, the Canadian government heavily rewards bilingualism. Taking both the IELTS (General Training) and the TEF Canada can add up to 50 additional CRS points to your profile.

Why Choose IELTS Over CELPIP?

While CELPIP is another accepted English test for Canada, it is not available in Algeria. Algerian candidates must sit for the IELTS General Training. The British Council offers computer-delivered tests in major cities like Algiers and Oran.

Target CLB 9 (Canadian Language Benchmark)

To maximize your points, you should aim for CLB 9 across all four skills. For the IELTS, this translates to:

  • Listening: 8.0
  • Reading: 7.0
  • Writing: 7.0
  • Speaking: 7.0
