How to Register & Pay for the British Council IELTS in Algeria

Registering for the IELTS in Algeria presents a unique challenge: the lack of accessible international credit cards for many candidates. This guide walks you through the official British Council registration process and offline payment methods. our premier IELTS preparation in Oran facility provides the tactical blueprint to secure your target band.

Step 1: Create a British Council Account

Navigate to the official British Council Algeria website. You will need to select between Academic (for study) and General Training (for immigration). Make sure you also select the 'Computer-delivered' option for faster results.

Step 2: Upload Identification

You must upload a clear scan of a valid passport or national ID card. The ID you register with must be the exact same physical ID you bring on test day.

Step 3: Offline Payment via Bank Transfer

Because many Algerians cannot pay online via Visa/Mastercard, the British Council allows offline bank transfers. Once you reserve your seat, you usually have 3 to 5 days to complete the payment via a local bank (such as Société Générale Algérie) to the British Council's designated account.

Always include your reference number in the transfer motif, and email the payment receipt to the British Council immediately.
