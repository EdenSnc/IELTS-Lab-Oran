How to Register for the British Council IELTS in Algeria

Quick Summary:

The test fee in Algeria is currently 40,000 DA. You must register through the official British Council Portal. Payment must be made offline within 3-5 days of online registration, usually via a bank transfer (CPA Bank) or at a local British Council center.

Registering for the IELTS in Algeria can seem daunting, but the British Council has streamlined the process significantly in recent years. Here is exactly what you need to know to secure your test date without issues.

Step-by-Step Registration

1

Create a British Council Account

Go to the official British Council Algeria website. You will need to create a profile using exactly the same name and details that appear on your valid Passport or National ID card.

2

Choose Format and Location

You must select whether you are taking Academic or General Training. Then, select your format: Computer-delivered or Paper-based. Test centers are available in Algiers, Oran, and Constantine.

3

Upload ID Document

You must upload a clear, color scan of your ID. Crucial Rule: The ID you use to register is the exact same ID you must bring on the day of the test. If you register with your passport, do not bring your national ID card to the test center.

4

Offline Payment

After booking, your seat is temporarily reserved. You usually have 3 to 5 days to complete the payment. You can pay via a direct bank transfer (usually to CPA Bank) using the references provided in your confirmation email, or in cash at an authorized center.

Computer-Delivered vs Paper-Based

We strongly recommend the Computer-Delivered test. Not only are the results available much faster (3-5 days instead of 13 days), but the interface provides a massive advantage for Algerian candidates:

  • Word Count: The screen automatically counts your words in the Writing section.
  • Editing: You can copy, paste, and rewrite sentences instantly without messy erasers.
  • Timers: A persistent on-screen clock flashes red when you are low on time.
  • Headphones: Everyone gets their own high-quality headphones for the Listening section (no echoey rooms).

Prepare Before You Pay

40,000 DA is a significant investment. Do not book your test date until you are consistently hitting your target band scores in realistic, timed, computer-based practice sessions.
